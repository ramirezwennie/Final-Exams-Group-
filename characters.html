Meet the Characters

The heroes and villains of New York City.

Protagonist

Papa Smurf

The wise leader of the Smurfs, recognizable by his red clothing and white beard. He uses alchemy to protect his village.

Voice Actor: Jonathan Winters

Protagonist

Smurfette

The only female Smurf in the village. Brave and kind-hearted, she keeps the boys in check.

Voice Actor: Katy Perry

Protagonist

Brainy Smurf

Bookish and pedantic, often annoying others with his unwanted advice, but he always means well.

Voice Actor: Fred Armisen

Protagonist

Clumsy Smurf

Lovable but accident-prone. His clumsiness often leads to trouble, but sometimes it saves the day.

Voice Actor: Anton Yelchin

Protagonist

Gutsy Smurf

Brave, bold, and wearing a kilt, Gutsy is the action hero who is never afraid of a fight.

Voice Actor: Alan Cumming

Antagonist

Gargamel

The evil wizard who pursues the Smurfs into New York City to capture their magical essence.

Actor: Hank Azaria

Antagonist

Azrael

Gargamel's intelligent cat. He often laughs at Gargamel's failures but remains loyal to the chase.

Voice Actor: Frank Welker
