The Smurfs (2011)

A live-action/animated family comedy where the Smurfs are transported to modern-day New York City and must find a way home while avoiding the evil wizard Gargamel.

Highlights

  • Family-friendly comedy mixing live-action and animation
  • Colorful characters and physical humor
  • Great for kids and nostalgic adults
  • Soundtrack featuring lively scores

Synopsis

The Smurfs are chased from their village by the wicked Gargamel and accidentally transported to 21st-century New York City. Lost in a big city they don't understand, the Smurfs must work together with new human friends to find a way back to their world before Gargamel captures them.
